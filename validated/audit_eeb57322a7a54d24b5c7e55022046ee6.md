### Title
Unchecked Felt Field Arithmetic Overflow in `compute_max_possible_fee` Allows Fee-Free Transaction Execution — (File: `execution/transaction_impls.cairo`)

---

### Summary

`compute_max_possible_fee` in `transaction_impls.cairo` performs unchecked multiplication of `max_amount` and `max_price_per_unit` in the Cairo felt field. Because `max_price_per_unit` carries no upper-bound constraint (only `assert_nn`, which permits values up to `(P−1)/2 ≈ 2^250`), the product can silently wrap modulo the STARK prime P. A user can craft V3 resource bounds that cause the computed `max_fee` to equal exactly 0, triggering the early-return guard in `charge_fee` and executing the transaction without paying any fee.
