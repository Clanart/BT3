### Title
Child `ChunkExtra.proposed_split` Inherits Parent's Non-`None` Value During Shard Split, Causing `InvalidChunkHeaderShardSplit` for Every Child-Shard Chunk — (File: chain/chain/src/resharding/manager.rs)

### Summary

In `process_memtrie_resharding_storage_update`, the child `ChunkExtra` is built by cloning the parent's `ChunkExtra` and overwriting only `state_root` and `congestion_info`. The `proposed_split` field — which holds the `TrieSplit` that triggered the resharding — is never reset to `None`. Because the parent's last chunk always carries `proposed_split = Some(TrieSplit{…})`, every child shard starts life with that stale value committed to `DBCol::ChunkExtra`.
