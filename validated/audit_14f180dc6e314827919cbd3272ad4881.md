### Title
Child `ChunkExtra.proposed_split` Inherited from Parent Causes `InvalidChunkHeaderShardSplit` on First Child Chunk After Resharding — (File: `chain/chain/src/resharding/manager.rs`)

---

### Summary

During resharding, the child shard's `ChunkExtra` is cloned from the parent's `ChunkExtra` without resetting the `proposed_split` field. When `min_epochs_between_resharding = 1` (the documented minimum valid value), the parent can still propose a split in the transition epoch (N+1), so the child's `ChunkExtra` at the resharding block inherits `proposed_split = Some(TrieSplit{…})`. The child's first chunk header then computes `proposed_split = None` (freshly split shard is below threshold), and the strict equality check in `validate_chunk_with_chunk_extra_and_receipts_root` fires `InvalidChunkHeaderShardSplit`, halting the child shard.
