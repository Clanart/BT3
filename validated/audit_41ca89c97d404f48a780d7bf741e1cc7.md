### Title
Relayer fee permanently frozen in bridge when fast-transfer-to-Near with message fails — (`near/omni-bridge/src/lib.rs`)

---

### Summary

When a fast transfer to a NEAR recipient that includes a message (`msg`) fails because the recipient's `ft_on_transfer` rejects the tokens, `resolve_fast_transfer` removes the fast-transfer record and refunds only `amount_without_fee` to the relayer. The fee portion of the tokens the relayer deposited is never returned and is permanently locked inside the bridge contract.

---

### Finding Description

**Vulnerability class:** callback/state desync — pending fee claim is discarded when the fast-transfer record is burned/removed without first settling the outstanding fee obligation.

**Root cause — `resolve_fast_transfer`**

In `fast_fin_transfer_to_near_callback` the relayer deposits `amount` tokens (principal + fee) into the bridge via `ft_transfer_call`. The bridge then calls `send_tokens(token, recipient, amount_without_fee, &msg)`. Because `msg` is non-empty, `send_tokens` issues a `mint(recipient, amount_without_fee, Some(msg))` (deployed token) or `ft_transfer_call(recipient, amount_without_fee, msg)` (native token). If the recipient's `ft_on_transfer` rejects the tokens (returns the full amount), the promise result is `U128(0)` (zero tokens actually transferred).
