### Title
Unchecked Fee Arithmetic Result Exceeds `assert_nn_le` Range Bound, Causing OS Execution Failure — (File: `crates/apollo_starknet_os_program/src/cairo/starkware/starknet/core/os/execution/transaction_impls.cairo`)

---

### Summary

`compute_max_possible_fee` multiplies attacker-controlled `max_amount` (bounded to `[0, 2^64 - 1]`) by `max_price_per_unit` (bounded only to `[0, 2^128 - 1]`) without validating that the resulting product fits within the range expected by the downstream `assert_nn_le` call. The product can reach ~`3 × 2^192`, far exceeding `2^128 - 1`. When this oversized `max_fee` is passed to `assert_nn_le(calldata.amount.low, max_fee)`, the internal `assert_le` range-check constraint fails unconditionally, aborting OS execution and making the block unprovable.
