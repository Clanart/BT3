### Title
Stale `feemarketParams` Snapshot in `newCosmosAnteHandler` Allows Cosmos Transactions to Bypass Current Base Fee and `MinGasPrice` Enforcement — (File: `evmd/ante/handler_options.go`)

---

### Summary

`newCosmosAnteHandler` captures `evmParams` and `feemarketParams` **once at construction time** and passes stale pointers into both `NewDynamicFeeChecker` and `MinGasPriceDecorator`. Because the EIP-1559 base fee is updated every block and `MinGasPrice` can be raised via governance, both fee-validation paths silently operate on outdated values for the entire lifetime of the handler. The analogous EVM path (`newEthAnteHandler`) correctly re-fetches params on every invocation, making the asymmetry a clear implementation defect.
